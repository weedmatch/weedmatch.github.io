At WeedMatch, we are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable German and European Union privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

To provide the WeedMatch experience, we may collect information that you provide directly, information generated through your use of the app, and information required to provide our services.

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Profile photos and other content you choose to upload
  • Approximate location
  • Messages sent through the app
  • Strain information and posts you create
  • Usage and analytics data to improve the app

Some information, such as profile photos, location preferences, and strain information, is provided voluntarily by users to enable specific WeedMatch features. Users can choose what information they share through their profile settings.

When you grant permission to access your device’s storage, WeedMatch only accesses files that you choose to upload. Your photos and other personal files remain on your device unless you intentionally upload them to the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Help you discover nearby users through approximate location
  • Enable messaging and matching features
  • Display your profile and strain posts to other users
  • Improve the performance, security, and user experience of the app
  • Develop new features and services
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

With your consent, we may also send you updates or promotional content related to WeedMatch.

Data Retention

Chat messages are retained for 7 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Other account information is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services or meet legal obligations.

Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account at any time by using the Delete Account button located in the My Account section of the app.

When your account is deleted, your personal information will be removed from our systems, except where we are legally required to retain certain data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Supabase, our cloud database and backend service provider, for securely storing and processing application data.
  • Government authorities or law enforcement agencies where required by applicable law.
  • Trusted service providers that assist in operating WeedMatch under strict confidentiality obligations.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including secure data storage, encryption where appropriate, and regular security reviews.

Although we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and should notify us immediately if they suspect unauthorized access to their account.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email address below.

We will respond to requests regarding your personal data within the timeframes required by applicable data protection laws.

Age Requirement

WeedMatch is intended solely for individuals 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.
