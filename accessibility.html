Accessibility Statement

At WeedMatch, we are committed to ensuring our platform is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 and applicable European accessibility laws, including the European Accessibility Act (EAA).

Accessibility Features

WeedMatch includes the following accessibility features:

  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Our platform is designed to work with commonly used screen readers.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Users can navigate the application using only a keyboard without relying on a mouse.
  • Color Contrast: We use high-contrast colors to improve readability for users with visual impairments.
  • Resizable Text: Text can be resized or zoomed without losing content or functionality.
  • Responsive Design: The platform is designed to function across different screen sizes and devices.
  • Clear Navigation: Consistent layouts and clearly labeled controls help users navigate the app more easily.

Our Commitment

While we have made significant progress in improving accessibility, we recognize that some areas of the platform may still require enhancement.

WeedMatch is committed to regularly reviewing, testing, and improving accessibility to ensure an inclusive experience for all users.
